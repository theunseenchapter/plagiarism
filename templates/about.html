About Our AI Plagiarism Detector

How It Works

Our plagiarism detector uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and machine learning algorithms to analyze single texts for plagiarism indicators.

The system employs multiple sophisticated approaches to detect potential copying:

  • Pattern Recognition: Identifies suspicious patterns that commonly appear in copied content
  • Sentence Structure Analysis: Detects unusual sentence lengths and structures that may indicate copying
  • Academic Phrase Detection: Identifies overuse of common academic phrases and expressions
  • Text Statistics Analysis: Analyzes word diversity, sentence complexity, and other linguistic markers
  • Content Source Indicators: Looks for references, citations, and source-related keywords
